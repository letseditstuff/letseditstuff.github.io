This website was built on caffeine and love. But are those two really any different?

As a senior English major, for my Editing 3720 course we were tasked with finding an "ugly document," and editing it to perfection. After pondering for some time as to what I should fix, I realized the answer had been in front of me the whole time.

What's uglier than a text book?

If I was going to edit the shit out of something, and redesign it, I was sure as hell gonna make sure it was something that I was actually going to use. So, I took the reference pages of editors marks from the course's texts, and created the website you see here.

Feel free to use this page, and if you're feeling especially appreciative, you can donate to the Caffeine Fund.
